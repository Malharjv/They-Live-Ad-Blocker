THEY LIVE AD BLOCKER

Privacy Policy

Overview

They Live Ad Blocker does not collect, sell, or share personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

What we store locally

The extension saves one setting on your device: whether sunglasses (ad replacement) are enabled or disabled. This is stored using Chrome’s local storage and never leaves your browser.

What we access

When sunglasses are enabled, the extension reads the structure of the current web page to identify display advertisements and replace them with They Live-style messages. This happens entirely on your device.

  • We do not record browsing history.
  • We do not track clicks, keystrokes, or scroll behavior.
  • We do not send page content to any server.

Third parties

We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or any third-party data services.
